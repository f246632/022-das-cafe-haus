Welcome to Das Cafe Haus

A cozy Berlin café in the heart of Wedding, where tradition meets comfort.

Das Cafe Haus has been serving the local community with delicious breakfast options, aromatic coffee, and homemade treats. Whether you're looking for a quiet spot to work on your laptop, enjoying a hearty Turkish breakfast, or treating yourself to our famous ice cream, we provide a warm and welcoming atmosphere.

Our commitment to quality and friendly service has made us a beloved neighborhood gathering place. Stop by for fresh baguettes, generous portions of ice cream at unbeatable prices, or our specialty covered apple cake with cream.
